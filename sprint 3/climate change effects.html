Major Effects of Climate Change

Climate change has widespread and long-term impacts on the environment, human health, and global economies.

1. Rising Temperatures

Increased greenhouse gases cause global temperatures to rise, leading to heatwaves, droughts, and water shortages.

2. Melting Ice and Sea-Level Rise

Polar ice caps and glaciers are melting, causing sea levels to rise and threatening coastal communities.

3. Extreme Weather Events

Climate change intensifies storms, floods, and wildfires, affecting millions of people and ecosystems worldwide.
