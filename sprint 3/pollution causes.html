Main Causes of Pollution

Pollution is caused by human activities that release harmful substances into the environment, contaminating the air, water, and soil. It affects all living beings and natural systems on Earth.

1. Industrial Emissions

Factories release pollutants such as carbon monoxide, sulfur dioxide, and heavy metals into the air and water, creating smog and acid rain.

2. Transportation

Cars, planes, and ships emit large quantities of greenhouse gases and particulate matter that degrade air quality.

3. Waste Disposal and Plastic Use

Improper waste management and the excessive use of plastics result in water and soil contamination, harming aquatic and land ecosystems.
