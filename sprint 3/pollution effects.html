Major Effects of Pollution

Pollution has widespread consequences for health, wildlife, and the climate. It weakens ecosystems, contributes to disease, and accelerates global warming.

1. Human Health Problems

Air pollution causes respiratory issues, heart diseases, and premature deaths. Water pollution leads to illnesses like cholera and dysentery.

2. Damage to Ecosystems

Chemical runoff and oil spills destroy aquatic habitats, while soil contamination reduces agricultural productivity.

3. Climate Change Acceleration

Greenhouse gases from pollution trap heat in the atmosphere, contributing to global warming and extreme weather events.
