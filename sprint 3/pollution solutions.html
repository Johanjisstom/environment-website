Solutions to Pollution

Reducing pollution requires global cooperation, technological innovation, and individual responsibility. Every small step contributes to a cleaner planet.

1. Transition to Renewable Energy

Using solar, wind, and hydro energy reduces dependence on fossil fuels and lowers greenhouse gas emissions.

2. Improve Waste Management

Recycling, composting, and reducing plastic use prevent waste buildup and protect ecosystems.

3. Promote Green Transportation

Using public transport, cycling, or electric vehicles helps reduce emissions and improve air quality in cities.
